Exam


The exam answers or choices have been rounded off (i.e., 117.3 may be shown as 120) and you should select the CLOSEST answer or choice to your actual answer. The closest choice may be greater or smaller than the actual answer. Do not round off your work and answers, you may need them for the following questions. Be sure to write down any equation, formula, etc and answer that you derive in your RESEARCH NOTEBOOK. Do not just plug numbers into your calculator. You must show all of your work and equations used on this practice exam or else you cannot get credit from your instructor. Because you will not be able to ask questions during the exam at the test center, please write down any assumptions or clarifications to the test that you have had to make on the practice exam in your RESEARCH NOTEBOOK.



Note that at different times of the practice exam, you may be asked to complete a survey and enter your participant ID. You may experience some delay as the program records your responses so we appreciate your patience in advance. At the end of the exam, please stay in your desk for about 20 minutes to allow your physiological levels to settle. You cannot use distractors during this time (e.g., cell phones) and soon after, a final survey will follow. Your practice session will take approximately 2.5 hours, about the time that you would have for the actual exam next week. Best of luck!
